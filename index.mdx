AI Puffer is a WordPress plugin for running AI features where the site is already managed: inside WordPress.

The plugin is organized around modules. Chatbots answer visitors. AI Forms turn submitted fields into generated output. Content Writer prepares posts, SEO content, and product copy. Automations run recurring work from sources such as RSS, CSV, Google Sheets, URLs, and WooCommerce. Image / Video Generator creates media. Usage & Billing records activity, limits access, and sells credits through WooCommerce.

Providers are separate from modules. OpenAI, Google, Azure, OpenRouter, DeepSeek, Ollama, Claude, ElevenLabs, Replicate, Pinecone, and Qdrant are connected once, then selected where a module needs them.

Knowledge is shared infrastructure. Posts, pages, products, files, URLs, sitemaps, RSS feeds, and uploaded documents can be indexed and reused by chatbots, forms, search, and automation tasks.

Usage controls are part of the system. Site owners can inspect logs, apply limits, price requests, create WooCommerce credit packages, and review activity by user, module, and source.

AI Puffer works with WordPress admin screens, shortcodes, blocks, users, products, logs, and permissions.
